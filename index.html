💭 Reflection Corner

Great job completing CivicQuest! Now take a moment to think deeply:

"In your own community, what is ONE civic action you can personally take right now to make it better — and WHY does it matter?"

🎓 Key Takeaways

Good citizens don't just know their rights — they act on their responsibilities. From voting to volunteering, every civic action strengthens our communities. History teaches us how decisions shaped the nation, and geography reminds us that our location connects us to larger stories.
